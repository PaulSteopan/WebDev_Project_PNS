About wheels of fire



My story

I’ve always had a passion for cars. Growing up, I spent countless hours with my dad in the garage, learning the ins and outs of engines and appreciating the craftsmanship that went into every vehicle. But it wasn’t just about the cars for me—it was about the stories they told, the feeling of owning something unique, and the thrill of the drive. After years in the corporate world, I realized I wasn’t fulfilled. I knew it was time to turn my passion into something bigger, something that could help others experience that same excitement. So, I took a bold step and started my car company.
